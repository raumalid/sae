ABOUT US

SAE NIT DURGAPUR CHAPTER

SAE INDIA collegiate chapter of NIT Durgapur was conceived in 2007 with an idea to integrate the students of the college into international fabric of automobile engineering. Presently 200 students are enjoying the benefit of SAE India membership.

Unfortunately most auto-enthusiasts gather the practical knowledge regarding automobiles and IC engines through automotive magazines and TV shows. Being a product of a reputable technical institute, we feel this source alone is insufficient as this leaves scores of students who have a liking in this field un-assuaged. Thus, in order to transform this liking into the power of knowledge we organise a number of innovative events, workshops and lectures round the year to provide a sacred space to acquire practical skills and a competitive platform where the sharpest minds battle in the quest to acheive distinction.

NIT DURGAPUR

A Google search of NIT Durgapur would lead you to its location, its history, its fantastic placement record etc... but not its heart. Because its heart resides within its students, and to look into it you need to to look through their eyes.

For the raw,basic facts NIT Durgapur is a premier technical college established in 1960 providing various degrees in various departments and preparing students for a brighter future. But the fact is, that it is not all this, but much more!! It is more than just a place bustling with intelligent minds in pursuit of a successful career. NIT Durgapur is an engine whose pistons are the qualified professors of this Institute, and which is fuelled by thhe limitless enthusiasm and unrestrained participation of its student body.

Future just seems exciting, not just bright and successful, and after 50 years in the process of nourishing students to become one of India's finest engineers, it's not surprising why NIT Durgapur is a dream. For the next 50 years to come, we are just revved up...
